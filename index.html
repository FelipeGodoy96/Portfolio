About me

I had my first contact wih the programming world at teenage when participating in the administration of an OTS and since there my curiosity and will power to understand how things work behind started to grow without limits.

As I was discovering myself and what things I liked, I did a technical course in biotechnology and worked in a pharmacy where I learned a lot about medications. I also worked on a callcenter where I learned to communicate better with people, be empathic and to solve them problem on the best way.
Later I worked as an Uber driver and started to think about my career and what would come next. So I decided that I want to become a programmer, to live again the dream of my youth and then in 2022 I concluded CTD course (Fullstack Web Developer) at Digital House/Rocketseat.
Since then I have been creating particular projects and working as a freelance more than a year developing web apps and sites.
My expertise includes ReactJS, Springboot, HTML, CSS, Sass, Java, JavaScript, MySQL, AWS, Docker.
